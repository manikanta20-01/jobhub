## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is it free to apply for jobs?**
A: Yes, applying for jobs on our platform is completely free for job seekers.

**Q: How do I know if a job is genuine?**
A: All job listings on our platform are verified by our team before being published.

**Q: Can I post a job for free?**
A: Yes, we offer free job postings for employers. Premium options are also available.

**Q: How long does it take to hear back after applying?**
A: Response times vary by employer, typically 1-2 weeks.
