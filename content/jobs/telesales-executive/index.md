## Job Description

QuickSell Insurance is a leading insurance distributor. We're hiring TeleSales Executives to sell insurance products to customers over phone.

You'll call potential customers, explain insurance products, address queries, and close sales. We provide comprehensive training on products and sales techniques.

This is a remote position with flexible timing options. High performers earn significant incentives.

## Eligibility

* Any graduate or 12th pass
* Good Hindi and English communication
* Persuasive speaking skills
* Basic computer knowledge
* Quiet workspace at home
* Freshers welcome

## Salary Details

* Fixed: ₹20,000 - ₹25,000/month
* Incentives: Up to ₹20,000/month additional
* Total earning potential: ₹45,000+/month
* Weekly and monthly contests
* Performance bonuses

## Selection Process

1. **Telephonic Screening**: Communication check
2. **Sales Aptitude**: Basic assessment
3. **Mock Call**: Sales simulation
4. **Training**: 1-week product training
5. **Go Live**: Start calling customers

## How to Apply

Apply with your resume and preferred working hours. Immediate joining available for selected candidates.
