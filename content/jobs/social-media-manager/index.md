## Job Description

BrandBuzz Marketing manages social media presence for leading consumer brands. We're seeking a creative Social Media Manager to develop and execute engaging content strategies.

You'll create content calendars, design posts, write captions, manage community interactions, run paid campaigns, and analyze performance metrics. Platforms include Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and emerging channels.

The role requires creativity, strategic thinking, and staying ahead of social media trends. You'll work with designers, copywriters, and client teams to deliver results.

## Eligibility

* 2+ years of social media management experience
* Proven track record of growing social accounts
* Proficiency in content creation tools (Canva, Photoshop)
* Experience with social media management tools (Hootsuite, Buffer)
* Understanding of social media analytics
* Excellent copywriting skills

## Salary Details

* Annual package: ₹5,00,000 - ₹9,00,000
* Performance-based incentives
* Creative tools subscriptions
* Flexible work arrangements
* Team creativity workshops

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: Social accounts managed
2. **Content Creation Test**: Create sample posts
3. **Strategy Presentation**: Present a content plan
4. **Campaign Analysis**: Review past campaign
5. **Final Interview**: Culture fit discussion

## How to Apply

Share links to social media accounts you've managed, your portfolio, and resume. Include analytics showing growth and engagement metrics.
