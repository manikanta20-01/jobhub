## Job Description

SalesForce India is expanding its business development team. We're looking for motivated individuals to join as Business Development Executives and drive growth for our enterprise software solutions.

This role involves prospecting new clients, understanding their business needs, conducting product demonstrations, and closing deals. You'll receive comprehensive training on B2B sales methodologies and our product suite.

This is an excellent opportunity to build a career in enterprise sales with one of the world's leading CRM companies. High performers have fast-track growth opportunities.

## Eligibility

* MBA or graduate in Business, Marketing, or related field
* Strong communication and presentation skills
* Self-motivated with a results-driven attitude
* Willingness to travel (30-40%)
* Freshers with strong interpersonal skills welcome

## Salary Details

* Base salary: ₹5,00,000 - ₹10,00,000 per year
* Uncapped commission: 10-20% of deal value
* Average OTE (On-Target Earnings): ₹12,00,000+
* Company car and fuel allowance for client meetings
* Mobile and laptop provided

## Selection Process

1. **Application Screening**: Resume evaluation
2. **Aptitude Test**: Sales aptitude and logical reasoning
3. **Group Discussion**: Communication and teamwork assessment
4. **Role Play**: Mock sales pitch
5. **HR Interview**: Final evaluation

## How to Apply

Submit your resume with a cover letter explaining why you're interested in sales. Include any achievements that demonstrate your drive and results orientation.
