## Job Description

WebWizards Agency creates WordPress websites for businesses. We're hiring WordPress Developers for custom theme development and plugin customization.

You'll build responsive websites, customize themes, develop plugins, integrate WooCommerce, and optimize site performance. Experience with page builders like Elementor and custom PHP development is valued.

We offer both full-time and project-based opportunities. Remote work with flexible hours.

## Eligibility

* 2+ years of WordPress development
* Strong PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript skills
* Experience with custom theme development
* WooCommerce and plugin customization knowledge
* Understanding of SEO best practices

## Salary Details

* Full-time: ₹5,00,000 - ₹10,00,000/year
* Project-based: Negotiable per project
* Plugin/theme sales revenue share
* Flexible working arrangements
* Equipment allowance

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: WordPress sites built
2. **Coding Test**: Custom functionality task
3. **Technical Interview**: WordPress expertise
4. **Project Discussion**: Scope understanding
5. **Offer and Onboarding**

## How to Apply

Send links to WordPress sites you've built, GitHub/code samples, and your resume. Mention your availability and preferred work arrangement.
