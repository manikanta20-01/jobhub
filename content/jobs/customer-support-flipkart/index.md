## Job Description

Flipkart is hiring Customer Support Associates for remote work positions. This role involves handling customer queries and concerns through chat and email channels. You'll be the voice of Flipkart, helping customers with order tracking, returns, refunds, and general inquiries.

Working from the comfort of your home, you'll be part of a distributed team that provides 24/7 support to millions of Flipkart customers across India. The company provides all necessary equipment including a laptop and headset.

This position offers flexible shift timings and is perfect for fresh graduates, homemakers returning to work, or anyone seeking a stable remote job with a reputed Indian company.

## Eligibility

* Any graduate or undergraduate (final year students not eligible)
* Excellent written communication skills in English
* Hindi or regional language proficiency is a plus
* Basic computer knowledge and typing speed of 30+ WPM
* Quiet workspace at home with reliable internet connection
* Freshers welcome

## Salary Details

* Monthly salary: ₹25,000 - ₹40,000
* Performance incentives up to ₹5,000/month
* Internet allowance: ₹1,500/month
* Health insurance provided
* Paid training for 2 weeks

## Selection Process

1. **Online Application**: Submit your details
2. **Communication Test**: Written English assessment
3. **Voice/Chat Assessment**: Mock customer interaction
4. **System Check**: Verify home setup meets requirements
5. **Final Interview**: HR round
