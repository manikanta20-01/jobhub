## Job Description

Google is seeking talented Software Engineers to join our team in Bangalore. As a Software Engineer at Google, you will work on complex computer science problems, develop scalable software solutions, and contribute to products that impact billions of users worldwide.

You will be part of a collaborative team environment where innovation and creativity are encouraged. The role involves designing, developing, testing, deploying, maintaining, and improving software applications. You will work with cutting-edge technologies including machine learning, cloud computing, and distributed systems.

Our engineers work on projects that span across Google's product areas including Search, Ads, YouTube, Maps, and Cloud. This is an excellent opportunity to grow your technical skills while working with some of the brightest minds in the industry.

## Eligibility

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related technical field
* 3+ years of experience in software development
* Strong proficiency in one or more programming languages (Java, Python, C++, Go)
* Experience with data structures, algorithms, and software design
* Excellent problem-solving skills
* Strong communication and teamwork abilities

## Salary Details

The compensation package includes:
* Base salary: ₹25,00,000 - ₹45,00,000 per year
* Performance bonus: Up to 20% of base salary
* Stock options and RSUs
* Health insurance for you and your family
* Relocation assistance if applicable

## Selection Process

1. **Online Assessment**: Coding test and technical MCQs
2. **Technical Phone Screen**: 45-minute interview with a Google engineer
3. **On-site/Virtual Interviews**: 4-5 rounds covering coding, system design, and behavioral questions
4. **Hiring Committee Review**: Final evaluation by senior engineers
5. **Offer**: Successful candidates receive offer within 2 weeks
