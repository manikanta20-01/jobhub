## Job Description

InfoTech Data Solutions processes large volumes of data for clients in healthcare, retail, and finance sectors. We need reliable Data Entry Operators to work remotely on data digitization projects.

Work involves entering data from scanned documents into digital formats, verifying accuracy, and meeting daily productivity targets. Training will be provided on our data entry software and quality standards.

This is an ideal position for those seeking flexible work-from-home opportunities. You can choose your working hours as long as weekly targets are met.

## Eligibility

* 10th/12th pass or any graduate
* Basic computer skills (MS Office)
* Typing speed of 40+ WPM
* Attention to detail and accuracy
* Reliable internet connection
* Freshers welcome

## Salary Details

* Monthly earnings: ₹18,000 - ₹25,000
* Pay per entry model available
* Weekly payments
* Performance incentives
* No investment required (genuine job)

## Selection Process

1. **Online Registration**: Submit your details
2. **Typing Test**: Speed and accuracy assessment
3. **Sample Task**: Complete a small data entry task
4. **Document Verification**: ID and address proof
5. **Onboarding**: Account setup and training
