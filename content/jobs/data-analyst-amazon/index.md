## Job Description

Amazon India is looking for enthusiastic Data Analysts to join our growing analytics team in Hyderabad. This is an excellent entry-level opportunity for recent graduates who are passionate about data and want to start their career with a world-class technology company.

As a Data Analyst, you will work closely with business teams to understand their data needs, build reports and dashboards, perform ad-hoc analyses, and help drive data-driven decision making across the organization. You will get hands-on experience with big data technologies and learn how analytics powers one of the world's largest e-commerce platforms.

This role offers tremendous learning opportunities with mentorship from senior analysts and data scientists. You'll gain exposure to various aspects of Amazon's business including retail, operations, and customer experience.

## Eligibility

* Bachelor's degree in any discipline (BE/BTech, BSc, BCom, BA, etc.)
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
* Proficiency in Excel and SQL (basic to intermediate)
* Knowledge of Python or R is a plus
* Good communication skills in English
* Freshers are encouraged to apply

## Salary Details

* Annual CTC: ₹8,00,000 - ₹15,00,000
* Joining bonus: ₹50,000
* Medical insurance coverage
* Employee discount on Amazon purchases
* Learning and development programs

## Selection Process

1. **Online Application**: Submit resume and cover letter
2. **Aptitude Test**: Logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude, and basic SQL
3. **Technical Assessment**: Data interpretation and case study
4. **HR Interview**: Culture fit and communication skills
5. **Final Offer**: Selected candidates receive offer letter

## How to Apply

Apply directly through this job posting or visit amazon.jobs. Create your candidate profile and submit your application. Make sure your resume highlights any data-related projects or coursework.
