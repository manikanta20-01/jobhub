## Job Description

FinanceFirst Advisors provides accounting and financial services to small and medium businesses. We're looking for an experienced Accountant to manage client accounts and ensure compliance with tax regulations.

Responsibilities include maintaining books of accounts, preparing financial statements, filing GST returns, TDS compliance, and assisting with audits. You'll work with multiple clients across various industries.

This role offers exposure to diverse business scenarios and the opportunity to develop expertise in Indian taxation and accounting standards.

## Eligibility

* B.Com, M.Com, or CA Inter
* 1-3 years of accounting experience
* Proficiency in Tally ERP 9 and/or Tally Prime
* Knowledge of GST, TDS, and Income Tax
* Attention to detail and accuracy

## Salary Details

* Annual salary: ₹3,00,000 - ₹5,50,000
* Performance bonus based on client satisfaction
* Professional development support
* Health insurance coverage
* Festival bonuses

## Selection Process

1. **Application Review**: Credentials verification
