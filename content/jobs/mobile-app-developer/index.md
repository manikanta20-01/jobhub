## Job Description

AppInnovate Technologies develops popular consumer mobile applications. We're looking for React Native Developers to build and maintain high-performance cross-platform apps.

You'll work on features that impact millions of users, optimize app performance, implement complex UI animations, and ensure smooth integration with backend APIs. Experience with both iOS and Android deployment is essential.

Our apps are featured regularly on App Store and Play Store. Join us to create products that people love to use every day.

## Eligibility

* 2+ years of React Native development
* Published apps on App Store/Play Store
* Strong JavaScript/TypeScript skills
* Experience with Redux/MobX state management
* Knowledge of native modules and bridges

## Salary Details

* Annual CTC: ₹8,00,000 - ₹16,00,000
* App performance bonuses
* Latest MacBook and devices
* App Store featured bonus
* Learning and development budget

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: Your published apps
