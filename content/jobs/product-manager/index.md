## Job Description

ProductLeap Innovations builds B2B SaaS products. We're hiring Product Managers to define product vision, prioritize features, and deliver successful products to market.

You'll conduct user research, analyze market trends, create roadmaps, write user stories, and work closely with engineering and design teams. The role requires balancing stakeholder needs with technical constraints.

This is a high-impact role where your decisions shape the product direction and business outcomes.

## Eligibility

* 4+ years of product management experience
* Experience with agile methodologies
* Strong analytical and data-driven mindset
* Excellent communication and stakeholder management
* MBA or engineering background preferred

## Salary Details

* CTC: ₹18,00,000 - ₹35,00,000/year
* ESOPs available
* Performance bonus
* Leadership development programs
* Premium health benefits

## Selection Process

1. **Resume Review**: Experience evaluation
2. **Case Study**: Product scenario analysis
3. **Product Presentation**: Present your approach
4. **Stakeholder Simulation**: Role-play exercise
5. **Leadership Interview**: Final discussion

Send resume with details of products managed, metrics improved, and your product philosophy. Include LinkedIn profile.
