## Job Description

GrowthHack Media, a leading digital marketing agency in Mumbai, is looking for enthusiastic Digital Marketing Interns. This 6-month internship program is designed to give you hands-on experience in all aspects of digital marketing including SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, and paid advertising.

As an intern, you'll work on real client projects under the guidance of experienced marketers. You'll learn to create marketing strategies, manage social media accounts, write engaging content, analyze campaign performance, and use industry tools like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, and SEMrush.

This internship offers a pathway to a full-time position based on performance. Many of our current full-time employees started as interns!

## Eligibility

* Currently pursuing or recently completed graduation in any field
* Strong interest in digital marketing and social media
* Good writing and communication skills
* Basic knowledge of Canva, Photoshop, or design tools is a plus
* Self-motivated and eager to learn
* Available for at least 6 months

## Salary Details

* Monthly stipend: ₹15,000 - ₹25,000
* Performance bonus
* Certificate of completion
* Letter of recommendation for top performers
* Potential full-time offer after internship

## Selection Process

1. **Application Review**: Resume screening
2. **Written Test**: Basic marketing knowledge and creative writing
3. **Portfolio Review**: Share any social media accounts or content you've created
4. **Interview**: Discussion about your interests and goals
5. **Selection**: Offer letter to selected candidates

## How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief cover letter explaining why you're interested in digital marketing. Include links to any social media accounts you manage or content you've created.
