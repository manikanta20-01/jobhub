## Job Description

HealthInfo Solutions provides medical coding services to hospitals and clinics. We're hiring Medical Coders to translate healthcare procedures into standardized codes for billing and insurance.

You'll review medical records, assign ICD and CPT codes, ensure coding accuracy, and maintain compliance with regulations. Training provided for freshers.

This is a stable career option in healthcare with opportunities to become certified and advance.

## Eligibility

* Life sciences graduate (B.Sc, B.Pharm, etc.)
* Good English language skills
* Attention to detail
* CPC certification preferred (training provided)
* Basic computer skills
* Freshers welcome

## Salary Details

* CTC: ₹3,50,000 - ₹6,50,000/year
* CPC certification bonus
* Performance incentives
* Health insurance
* Work-from-home options

## Selection Process

1. **Aptitude Test**: Attention to detail
2. **Medical Terminology**: Basic assessment
3. **Interview**: Communication and fit
4. **Training**: 2-4 week paid training
5. **Certification**: CPC exam support

## How to Apply

Apply with resume mentioning educational background and any healthcare or coding experience. Life sciences graduates preferred.
