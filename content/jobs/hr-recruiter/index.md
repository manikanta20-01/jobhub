## Job Description

TalentConnect Solutions is a growing HR consulting firm helping companies find the right talent. We're hiring HR Recruiters to join our team and manage end-to-end recruitment for our clients.

You'll learn to source candidates through various channels, screen resumes, conduct interviews, coordinate with hiring managers, and manage the offer process. This role provides comprehensive exposure to recruitment best practices.

We provide extensive training in recruitment tools, interviewing techniques, and HR technologies. High performers can grow into Senior Recruiter or HR Business Partner roles.

## Eligibility

* MBA in HR or any graduate with interest in HR
* Good communication skills in English
* Basic computer proficiency
* People-oriented with strong interpersonal skills
* Freshers encouraged to apply

## Salary Details

* Annual CTC: ₹3,50,000 - ₹6,00,000
* Recruitment incentives per closure
* Work-from-home option available
* Professional certification support
* Annual performance bonus

## Selection Process

1. **Resume Screening**: Initial shortlisting
2. **Telephonic Interview**: Basic HR questions
3. **Written Test**: Communication and logical skills
4. **Face-to-Face Interview**: Detailed discussion
5. **Final Selection**: Offer rollout

## How to Apply

Apply with your resume and a brief statement about your interest in HR. Any relevant coursework or projects should be highlighted.
