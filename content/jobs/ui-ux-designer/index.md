## Job Description

DesignHub is a leading design agency serving Fortune 500 clients and innovative startups. We're seeking talented UI/UX Designers to create intuitive and visually stunning digital experiences.

You'll work on diverse projects including mobile apps, web applications, design systems, and brand identities. Collaboration with product managers, developers, and clients is a key part of this role.

The ideal candidate has a keen eye for detail, strong understanding of design principles, and stays updated with the latest design trends and tools.

## Eligibility

* 2+ years of UI/UX design experience
* Proficiency in Figma and Adobe Creative Suite
* Strong portfolio demonstrating your design process
* Understanding of user-centered design principles
* Experience with design systems and component libraries
* Good communication skills for client interactions

## Salary Details

* Annual CTC: ₹8,00,000 - ₹18,00,000
* Performance bonuses quarterly
* Latest design tools subscription provided
* Conference attendance budget
* Health and wellness benefits

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: Assessment of previous work
2. **Design Exercise**: Complete a take-home design challenge
3. **Presentation**: Present your solution to the team
4. **Interview**: Discussion of your design philosophy
5. **Final Offer**: For selected candidates

## How to Apply

Apply with your portfolio link, resume, and a brief introduction. Include case studies that show your problem-solving approach.
