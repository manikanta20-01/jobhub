## Job Description

MediaMax Productions creates video content for brands and influencers. We're hiring Video Editors to produce engaging content for YouTube, Instagram, and advertising campaigns.

You'll edit raw footage, add graphics and effects, color grade, mix audio, and deliver final cuts. Projects include vlogs, commercials, corporate videos, and social media content.

Creative storytelling and attention to pacing are crucial. You'll work with directors, content creators, and clients.

## Eligibility

* 2+ years of video editing experience
* Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects
* Strong portfolio showing editing skills
* Understanding of social media video formats
* Color grading and audio mixing skills
* Motion graphics ability is a plus

## Salary Details

* Salary: ₹4,00,000 - ₹9,00,000/year
* Project completion bonuses
* Adobe Creative Suite license
* High-performance editing workstation
* Flexible hours for remote editors

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: Previous video work
2. **Editing Test**: Edit a sample footage
3. **Technical Assessment**: Software proficiency
4. **Creative Discussion**: Approach to storytelling
5. **Final Interview**: Terms discussion

## How to Apply

Share your portfolio (YouTube channel, Vimeo, Drive link) with your best editing work. Include resume and software proficiencies.
