## Job Description

CloudServe Solutions is a cloud consulting partner helping businesses migrate to and manage cloud infrastructure. We're hiring Technical Support Engineers to assist our clients with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.

You'll troubleshoot technical issues, guide clients through cloud configurations, document solutions, and escalate complex problems. This is an excellent entry point into cloud computing careers.

We provide training and support for cloud certifications (AWS Cloud Practitioner, Azure Fundamentals, etc.). Many of our support engineers have grown into Cloud Architects and DevOps roles.

## Eligibility

* B.Tech/B.E. in CS/IT or related field
* Basic understanding of cloud concepts
* Good problem-solving abilities
* Excellent communication skills
* Willingness to learn new technologies
* Freshers welcome

## Salary Details

* CTC: ₹4,00,000 - ₹7,00,000 per year
* Certification reimbursement
* Shift allowances
* Performance bonuses
* Health insurance
