## Job Description

ContentPro is a content creation platform connecting talented writers with businesses needing quality content. We're looking for freelance content writers to join our pool of creators.

As a freelance writer, you'll have the freedom to choose projects that interest you. Work includes writing blog posts, website copy, product descriptions, social media content, and more. You can work from anywhere and set your own schedule.

We serve clients across various industries including technology, health, finance, travel, and lifestyle. Whether you're a beginner or experienced writer, we have projects suited to your skill level.

## Eligibility

* Excellent command of English grammar and vocabulary
* Ability to research and write on diverse topics
* Basic understanding of SEO principles
* Reliable internet connection
* No prior experience required
* Freshers and students welcome

## Salary Details

* Pay per article/word based on complexity
* Beginners: ₹500 - ₹800 per 1000 words
* Experienced: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 per 1000 words
* Weekly payments via UPI/bank transfer
* Bonus for consistent quality work

## Selection Process

1. **Application**: Submit writing samples or complete a sample assignment
2. **Skill Test**: Write a 500-word article on a given topic
3. **Editorial Review**: Our editors evaluate your submission
4. **Onboarding**: Approved writers get platform access

## How to Apply

Apply with 2-3 writing samples or write a fresh sample on "Benefits of Remote Work." Include your preferred writing niches and availability.
