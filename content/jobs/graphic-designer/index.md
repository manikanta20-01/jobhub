## Job Description

CreativeStudio Agency serves clients with branding and marketing design needs. We're hiring Graphic Designers to create visual content across print and digital media.

You'll design social media graphics, brochures, banners, logos, packaging, and marketing collateral. Working with brand guidelines, you'll bring client visions to life with creativity and precision.

This role offers variety in projects and industries. You'll collaborate with marketing teams, photographers, and other designers in a creative environment.

## Eligibility

* Degree/Diploma in Graphic Design or Fine Arts
* Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
* Strong portfolio demonstrating design skills
* Understanding of design principles and typography
* Ability to meet deadlines and handle feedback
* Freshers with strong portfolio welcome

## Salary Details

* Annual salary: ₹4,00,000 - ₹8,00,000
* Adobe Creative Cloud subscription
* Performance incentives
* Creative workshops
* Portfolio development support

## Selection Process

1. **Portfolio Review**: Design work assessment
2. **Design Test**: Create a sample design
3. **Software Test**: Photoshop/Illustrator task
4. **Creative Discussion**: Design approach and inspiration
5. **Final Selection**: Offer to selected candidates

## How to Apply

Submit your portfolio (Behance, Dribbble, PDF, or website) with your resume. Include a variety of work showing different styles and mediums.
