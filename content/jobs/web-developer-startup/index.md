## Job Description

TechVenture Labs is an early-stage startup building innovative SaaS products for the Indian market. We're looking for a Full Stack Web Developer to join our core team and help build scalable web applications.

You'll work on everything from database design to UI implementation. Our tech stack includes React, Next.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and AWS. You'll have significant ownership of features and direct impact on product direction.

This is a unique opportunity to experience startup culture, learn rapidly, and potentially grow into a leadership role as the company scales.

## Eligibility

* 1-3 years of experience in web development
* Strong JavaScript skills and modern frameworks (React/Vue/Angular)
* Experience with backend development (Node.js/Python)
* Knowledge of databases (SQL and NoSQL)
* Familiarity with Git and CI/CD
* Self-starter with entrepreneurial mindset

## Salary Details

* Base salary: ₹6,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per year
* Equity/ESOPs: 0.1% - 0.5% based on experience
* Flexible work hours and remote options
* Learning budget for courses and conferences
* Team outings and hackathons

## Selection Process

1. **Resume Screening**: Initial evaluation
2. **Technical Assignment**: Build a small full-stack application
3. **Code Review**: Walkthrough of your assignment
4. **System Design Discussion**: Architecture and scalability
5. **Founder Interview**: Culture and vision alignment

## How to Apply

Send your resume, GitHub profile, and links to projects you've built. Include a brief note about why you want to join a startup.
